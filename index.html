The gift of life

As of October 2020, there are more than 15,000 patients waiting for organ transplantation in Europe.

Currently, the number of donated organs is still far below the demand on the waiting list. More than 10,000 patients are still waiting for help.

Among the organs needed for transplantation, kidney, heart, liver, and lung are the four most in demand.
